Trade School Live

An interactive platform that delivers free trading simulations with real-time macro- and microeconomic events, built entirely from scratch in Python. The simulation reacts dynamically to events triggered every minute and to user actions such as buying and selling stocks. Players trade using virtual currency that resets weekly, with the top five ranked on a public leaderboard. Accessible on almost any device, the experience will be available through YouTube, Twitch, and TikTok (Coming Soon).
